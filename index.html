Welcome to Creativity in the Classroom

Creativity plays a critical role in early childhood education. It allows children to express themselves, develop problem-solving skills, and explore the world around them in a meaningful way. This website is dedicated to providing early childhood educators with resources and strategies to facilitate creativity across various curriculum areas.

Explore the different sections of the website to learn how you can integrate creativity into your teaching practices through Art, Drama and Puppetry, Movement and Music, Language and Literacy, Mathematics and Numeracy, Humanities and Social Sciences, Technologies, Science, and Engineering. Additionally, discover the benefits of using an integrated curriculum approach, such as STEM, STEAM, STREAM, and STEMIE, to foster creativity in young learners.
